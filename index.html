Instructions

The Get 'Er Done daily game points you to a reward for getting your top 5 priorities done, having some fun in the process. Have a good laugh each time you complete a to-do, and receive a nice reward when you finish all your tasks. Essentially, Get ‘er Done!

Check your to-dos off and get rewarded with some fun. Not checking off your to-dos equals no fun, as well as decreasing allowed fun time. Did we tell you everything is timed?

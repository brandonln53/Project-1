How to play

Instructions

Life gets in the way of productivity, right?! The Get 'Er Done challenge rewards you for getting done what you really should get done, having some fun in the process. Check your to-dos off and get rewarded with some fun. Not checking off your to-dos equals no fun, as well as decreasing allowed fun time. Did we tell you everything is timed?


Essentially, Get 'er Done! Make it through all your tasks for a nice reward. Good luck.
